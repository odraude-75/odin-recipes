Traditional Recipe

Description

Simple and easy to make, yet very rewarding both to prepare and to enjoy. This sauce is green and precious, like an emerald.

Ingredients

  • You’ll need three cups of packed fresh basil.
  • Cheese: grate your Parmesan by hand.
  • Oil: use a good quality olive oil.
  • Nuts: ½ cup of pine nuts.
  • Garlic: fresh garlic.

How to make

Couldn't be easier: add all the ingredients to a food processor or blender and blend until smooth.
